## Design System Used

### Colors
- Primary: Navy Blue (#0A2540)
- Accent: Purple (#635BFF)
- Background: Light (#F7F9FC)
- Surface: White (#FFFFFF)
- Text: Navy (#0A2540), Gray (#425466), Light Gray (#697386)

### Typography
- Headers: Bold, 20-32px
- Body: Regular/Medium, 14-16px
- Captions: 12px

### Components
- Button Height: 56px
- Border Radius: 10-14px
- Shadows: Subtle (0.04-0.12 opacity)
- Spacing: 4-32px scale

### Principles
- Clean, minimal design
- Professional banking/fintech aesthetic
- No gradients, no excessive emojis
- Consistent spacing and shadows
- Touch-friendly (48px+ touch targets)
- Clear visual hierarchy
